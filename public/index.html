          About GeoScout IQ

          Location Decision Intelligence for BFSI & FMCG in India. We fuse live Google Maps data, multi-source web signals and Gemini grounding agents into one decision in ~30 seconds.

          What makes it strong

          • Use-case aware: an ATM, a bank branch, a grocery store and a warehouse are scored on different things — footfall vs affluence vs logistics access.
          • Zone-level reasoning: click any zone for a fact-backed AI verdict — why to open there, or why not.
          • Grounded, not guessed: growth claims are backed by live Google Search citations you can open.
          • Granular heatmap: every ~460 m hex scored on demand, competition, access & future growth.

          Where the data comes from

          Google Maps Platform — Geocoding & Places (New): competitors, your sites, and use-case-specific nearby context (metro, malls, schools, highways, ports). Includes ratings, review counts, price level & open/closed status — used as a live footfall & "dying high-street" signal.
          WorldPop population — free, current (2020, 100 m grid) gridded population for India. Gives the real people-count & density of the searched area as the primary demand signal.
          Google Search via Gemini 2.5 — grounded research on metro projects, real-estate launches, SEZs, infrastructure & recent news, with clickable citations. Also powers the per-zone "open here / avoid" verdict.
          99acres (via Apify) — live property listings & ₹/sqft as a local affluence and supply signal.
          India Post — PIN code & district lookup.
          OpenStreetMap — background density of local places as a demand backdrop.

          Built for the Ganit Inc. ideathon · Data Speaks.

          Import your existing locations

          Upload your store list, branch directory, or competitor locations. AI auto-maps your column headers.

          📋 What to expect:
          • Supported formats: CSV, Excel (.xlsx, .xls), and JSON.
          • Name column: Required to identify and label your pins.
          • Coordinates preferred: Columns with Latitude/Longitude will plot instantly and precisely.
          • Address geocoding fallback: If coordinates are missing, we will automatically geocode the address to find the coordinates and plot the location (up to 50 addresses per import).
          • If data is missing: Rows without coordinates or a valid address will be listed in the preview but cannot be plotted.
