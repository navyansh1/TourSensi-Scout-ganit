    About TourSensi Scout

    AI-powered site selection for BFSI & FMCG in India. We fuse live Google Maps data, multi-source web signals and Gemini grounding agents into one decision in ~30 seconds.

    What makes it strong

    • Use-case aware: an ATM, a bank branch, a grocery store and a warehouse are scored on different things — footfall vs affluence vs logistics access.
    • Zone-level reasoning: click any zone for a fact-backed AI verdict — why to open there, or why not.
    • Grounded, not guessed: growth claims are backed by live Google Search citations you can open.
    • Granular heatmap: every ~460 m hex scored on demand, competition, access & future growth.

    Where the data comes from

    Google Maps Platform — Geocoding, Places (New): competitors, your sites, and nearby context (metro, malls, schools, highways, ports). Prices, ratings & business status where available.
    Google Search via Gemini 2.5 — grounded research on metro projects, real-estate launches, SEZs, infrastructure & recent news, with citations.
    99acres (via Apify) — live property listings & ₹/sqft as a local affluence and supply signal.
    Wikipedia & India Post — area context paragraph, PIN code & district.
    OpenStreetMap — background POI density as a demand backdrop.
